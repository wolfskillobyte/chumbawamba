BIO

Chumbawamba was an english rock band that drew on genres such as punk rock, pop, and folk.

Their anarcho-communist political leanings led them to have an irreverent attitude toward authority, and to espouse a variety of political and social causes including:

  • - animal rights
  • - pacifism
  • - class struggle
  • - Marxism
  • - feminism
  • - gay liberation
  • - pop culture
  • - anti-fascism
